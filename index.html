About QR Maker & Features

QR Code Generator

QR Maker is a professional browser-based design toolkit built to craft beautiful, customizable QR codes entirely in your browser.

Custom Appearance: Modify foreground colors, solid or transparent backgrounds, pixel styles, and corner marker shapes.
Logo & Icons: Add custom logos, choose from a selection of built-in system icons, or input any custom emoji of your choice.
Rich Formats: Export as PNG or vector SVG directly to your device.
Instant Sharing: Share QR codes via dynamic URLs that load your exact design instantly.
Web App Ready: Install to your device to design offline in a full-screen environment.
